Data

Datasets, interactive visualisations, and open-source tools from my research on international law and institutions.

  • Corpus of Resolutions: UN Security Council (CR-UNSC)
    A comprehensive, machine-readable corpus of all UN Security Council resolutions. With Seán Fobbe and Lorenzo Gasbarri.
  • Words of Power: UN Security Council Resolutions
    Companion dataset with full-text and metadata for computational analysis. With Seán Fobbe and Lorenzo Gasbarri.

Data Essays

The Law of Citations – UNSC Resolution Network
Interactive data essay: 2,488 resolutions, 12,399 citations. Explore the full network, bridge animations, and citation functions.
GenAI as an International Lawyer – Jessup Moot Court
Interactive data essay: 10 AI memorials, 2 LLMs, blindly graded by Jessup judges. Scores, categories, and judge detection rates.
Custom in the Making – CIL Formation Model
Interactive data essay: 100 state agents, 5 network topologies, phase boundaries. How observation structure, treaties, and cheap talk shape customary international law.
cjeu-py – CJEU Case Law Toolkit
Interactive data essay: 1,020 Grand Chamber decisions, 12,673 citations, 51 doctrinal communities. A Python pipeline from SPARQL to interactive citation networks.

Code

  • cjeu-py
    Python toolkit for empirical research on the Court of Justice of the European Union. Collects, parses, extracts citations, classifies, and visualises CJEU case law. pip install cjeu-py
  • PraxisBeliefABM
    Agent-based model of customary international law formation. 100 state agents, 5 network topologies, phase boundaries. Python / Mesa.
