Research & Publications

My research on international adjudication, judicial reasoning, and computational law has been published in leading peer-reviewed journals and by major university presses. I also build tools and datasets for large-scale legal analysis — from OCR pipelines for court archives and citation mining across judicial decisions, to AI-powered legal research systems and comprehensive corpora of international legal materials.

A comprehensive list is also available on my Google Scholar profile. I welcome inquiries from prospective PhD students with innovative projects in my areas of expertise.
