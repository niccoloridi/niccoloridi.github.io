Profile

I am a Senior Lecturer (Associate Professor) in Public International Law at The Dickson Poon School of Law, King's College London. I also serve as Associate Director of the Centre for International Governance and Dispute Resolution (CIGAD) and Associate Dean for Doctoral Studies.

I am interested in the authority and ultimate effect of international law, as well as the processes through which international law is produced and undergoes change. I do so by applying empirical and computational methodologies to both legal argumentation and actor behaviour.
